Why?

Data posted to forums is often lost in the depths of the internet. The goal here is to collect and present it in a more organized manner.

Who?

The data was gathered from various sources by kinzyjr and the web app was developed during spare time by 5am.

How can I contribute?

The best way to share your own observations is to make a post about them on the PalmTalk forum. Make a thread about your ongoing experience and share pictures of the outcome! Please include the palm species, general location, and the lowest temperature you recorded along with a description of any unusual weather events. Don't forget to tag kinzyjr!

Do you write code? Contribute to the app by submitting a pull request on GitHub.

Ideas, suggestions, and feedback about the app can be sent to 5am on the PalmTalk forum, while thoughts about the data should be directed to kinzyjr.

Data Sources

There is no affiliation between this app and any of the wonderful data sources.
