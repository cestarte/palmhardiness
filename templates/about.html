What is all this?

Palm enthusiasts the world over are sharing their observations online in forums. That's wonderful! But have you ever tried to recall the details you read in some thread a month ago? A large effort has been made to collect and organize this data by kinzyjr. This goal of this app is to provide a web interface on top of that collected data set to make it more easily accessible, with links back to the source for further reading.

The data was gathered from various sources by kinzyjr and the web app was developed during spare time by 5am.

How can I contribute?

There are a few ways to contribute to the project:

Share your own observations on the PalmTalk forum! Make a thread about your experience and include pictures.

Ideas, suggestions, and feedback about the app can be sent to 5am on the PalmTalk forum. Concerns about the data should be directed to kinzyjr.

Do you write code? Check out the github. It's very easy to run the app on your computer.
