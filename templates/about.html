What is all this?

Palm enthusiasts the world over are sharing their observations online in forums. The information is out there but it's scattered across various forum threads and sites. A large effort has been made to collect and organize this data by kinzyjr. This goal of this app is to provide a web interface on top of that aggregated data set for greater accessibility, with links back to the source for further reading.

The data was gathered from various sources by kinzyjr and the web app was developed during spare time by 5am.

How can I contribute?

Share your own observations on the PalmTalk forum! Make a thread about your experience and include pictures.
See the data overview of kinzyjr's post to learn more about making good observations.

Concerns about the data should be directed to kinzyjr. Suggestions or feedback about the web app can be sent to 5am on the PalmTalk forum.

Do you write code? Check out the github to dive into the python, Javascript, and SQLite from your computer.

Download

The latest cold hardiness master data is available as an Excel spreadsheet at https://www.palmtalk.org/forum/topic/61358-0000-cold-hardiness-observation-master-data/. The app codebase is available at github here https://github.com/cestarte/palmhardiness.
